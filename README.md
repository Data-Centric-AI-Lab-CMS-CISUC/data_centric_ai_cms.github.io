### <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/globe.svg" width="22" height="22" style="vertical-align: middle;"> About the Lab

Integrated within the Cognitive and Media Systems Group@CISUC, the Data-Centric AI Lab was established with the goal of motivating researchers to develop data-centered approaches that address multiple key dimensions, including data quality, data distribution, data representation, data collection and generation, data efficiency, data governance, as well as fairness and bias. The lab encourages researchers to pursue not only theoretical advancements but also the development of practical solutions applicable to both academic research and real-world industrial challenges.

In this context, the lab promotes an interdisciplinary and collaborative environment where novel methodologies can be designed, validated, and deployed across diverse application domains. By emphasizing a data-centric perspective, the group aims to improve the reliability, robustness, and transparency of AI systems, ensuring that models are better aligned with the intrinsic characteristics and complexities of the data they process.

Furthermore, the Data-Centric AI Lab is committed to bridging the gap between research and practice by promoting open science principles, including the development of publicly available datasets, software tools, and libraries. Through active collaboration with international partners and industry stakeholders, the lab seeks to advance responsible and sustainable AI, contributing to both scientific progress and societal impact.

In this GitHub repository, you will find an overview of the group’s main activities and achievements, including the research team, publications, software tools, libraries, datasets, as well as contributions to scientific event organization and ongoing projects.
