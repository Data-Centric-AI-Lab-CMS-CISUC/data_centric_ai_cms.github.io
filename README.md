## 🔬 Overview

Integrated within the **Cognitive and Media Systems (CMS)** group at **CISUC** (Centre for Informatics and Systems of the University of Coimbra), the **Data-Centric AI Lab** was established to motivate researchers to develop data-centered approaches addressing multiple key dimensions of modern AI.

Our goal is to move beyond model-centric approaches, focusing instead on the data itself to improve the **reliability, robustness, and transparency** of AI systems. We encourage both theoretical advancements and the development of practical solutions for academic and industrial challenges.

### 🎯 Key Research Dimensions

- **Data Quality & Distribution**
- **Data Representation & Collection**
- **Data Generation & Efficiency**
- **Data Governance**
- **Fairness & Bias Mitigation**

---

## 🤝 Collaborative Environment

We promote an interdisciplinary and collaborative environment where novel methodologies are designed, validated, and deployed across diverse domains. By emphasizing a data-centric perspective, we ensure that models are better aligned with the intrinsic characteristics and complexities of the data they process.

## 🔓 Open Science & Impact

The Data-Centric AI Lab is committed to bridging the gap between research and practice through **Open Science** principles. This includes:
- Development of publicly available datasets.
- Open-source software tools and libraries.
- Active collaboration with international partners and industry stakeholders.
