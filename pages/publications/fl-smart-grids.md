## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/file-text.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Abstract
This work presents a data-centric federated learning approach for anomaly detection in smart grids. We demonstrate how privacy-preserving collaborative learning can be effectively used to identify industrial control system failures and cyber-attacks without centralizing sensitive data.

## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/quote.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Full Citation
> Dylan Perdigão, Tiago Cruz, Paulo Simões, Pedro Henriques Abreu, **"Data-centric federated learning for anomaly detection in smart grids and other industrial control systems"**, *NOMS 2024-2024 IEEE network operations and management symposium*, 5 pages, 2024.
