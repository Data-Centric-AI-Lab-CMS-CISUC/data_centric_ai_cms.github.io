## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/file-text.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Abstract
We propose "Category-wise Fine-Tuning" to resist incorrect pseudo-labels in multi-label image classification with partial labels. This approach enhances the model's ability to handle ambiguous or partially labeled data in complex image classification tasks.

## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/quote.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Full Citation
> Chak Fong Chong, Xinyi Fang, Jielong Guo, Pedro Henriques Abreu, Yapeng Wang, Xu Yang, Wei Ke, Sio-Kei Im, **"Category-wise Fine-Tuning: Resisting incorrect pseudo-labels in multi-label image classification with partial labels"**, *Neurocomputing*, 647, 16 pages, 2025.
