## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/file-text.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Abstract
Federated learning (FL) is a promising paradigm for privacy-preserving collaborative machine learning. However, ensuring fairness across different groups in FL remains a significant challenge. This survey provides a comprehensive analysis of group fairness in FL, documenting its challenges, providing a taxonomy of current solutions, and identifying key directions for future research.

## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/quote.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Full Citation
> Teresa Salazar, Helder Araújo, Alberto Cano, Pedro Henriques Abreu, **"A survey on group fairness in federated learning: Challenges, taxonomy of solutions and directions for future research"**, *Artificial Intelligence Review*, 59(2), 81 pages, 2026.
