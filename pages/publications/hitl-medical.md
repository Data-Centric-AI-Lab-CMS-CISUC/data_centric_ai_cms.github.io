## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/file-text.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Abstract
We propose an interpretable human-in-the-loop process designed to improve medical image classification accuracy and trust. By incorporating expert feedback into the model's decision-making process, we achieve more reliable and explainable results in clinical settings.

## <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lucide-icons/lucide/main/icons/quote.svg" width="20" height="20" style="vertical-align: middle;"> Full Citation
> Joana Cristo Santos, Miriam Seoane Santos, Pedro Henriques Abreu, **"An interpretable human-in-the-loop process to improve medical image classification"**, *International Symposium on Intelligent Data Analysis (IDA)*, pp.179-190, 2024.
